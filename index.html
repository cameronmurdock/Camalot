Cameron Ely-Murdock

Systems design · infrastructure · coordination

I design and build systems for organizing people, work, and shared resources.

Most of my work sits between software architecture, operational design, and social infrastructure — places where technical decisions quietly determine whether coordination works or fails.

I’m interested in systems that are:

  • — usable without explanation
  • — resilient under real conditions
  • — shaped by context, not abstraction

This site is a snapshot of how I work and what I’m thinking about.
