Rivr.Social

Infrastructure for local coordination

Rivr.Social is a platform designed for neighborhoods, venues, worker collectives, mutual aid groups, and civic ecosystems — places where real coordination happens.

It helps people:

  • share resources,
  • organize events and work,
  • track contributions,
  • form groups with real accountability,
  • and build trust through participation, not performance.

What Makes Rivr Different

Rivr is built around use, not feeds.

  • No follower economy
  • No engagement farming
  • No algorithmic manipulation
Instead:
Clear objects: people, groups, events, tasks, resources
Transparent participation and contribution history
Local-first design with federated potential
Cooperative and commons-aligned economics

Design Principles

Rivr is guided by a few non-negotiables:
