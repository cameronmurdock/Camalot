Rivr.Social

A platform for neighborhoods, venues, and mutual aid groups where real coordination happens.

The Concept

Rivr is built for use, not feeds.

It is designed for places where participation and trust matter more than engagement metrics. It helps communities share resources, organize labor, and build accountability.

  • — No follower economy
  • — No algorithmic manipulation
  • — Transparent history

Core Principles

Local Sovereignty

Communities own their data and their decisions. Sovereignty is the foundation of trust.

Human Scale

Complexity should live in the system, not the user. We design for clarity over features.

Interoperability

Systems should talk without collapsing meaning. We build for the digital commons.

Durability

Tools should still make sense in ten years. We build for long-term stewardship.
