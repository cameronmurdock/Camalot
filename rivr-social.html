Rivr

Rivr is a project I founded to address a recurring problem in my work: there is very little usable infrastructure for local coordination.

It’s a practical system for organizing groups, events, tasks, and shared resources. Nothing novel in isolation — the value is in how the pieces are composed and constrained.

Rivr exists as a working environment to test ideas about:

  • — structure vs. flexibility
  • — participation without noise
  • — systems that don’t require constant moderation

It’s ongoing. It informs the rest of my work.
