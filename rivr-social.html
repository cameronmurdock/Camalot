Coordination Infrastructure

Rivr.Social

A platform for neighborhoods, venues, and mutual aid groups where real coordination happens.

Built for Use, Not Feeds

Rivr is designed for places where participation and trust matter more than engagement metrics. It helps communities share resources, organize labor, and build accountability.

The Difference

  • No follower economy or engagement farming
  • No algorithmic manipulation
  • Transparent participation history
  • Cooperative economics
Rivr is a response to the failure of platforms to serve the places they extract from.

Design Principles

Local Sovereignty

Communities own their data and their decisions. Sovereignty is the foundation of trust.

Human Scale

Complexity should live in the system, not the user. We design for clarity over features.

Interoperability

Systems should talk without collapsing meaning. We build for the digital commons.

Durability

Tools should still make sense in ten years. We build for the long-term stewardship of communities.
