Background & Philosophy

About
Cameron

Studying how people organize themselves—culturally, economically, and technically.

The Journey

I've spent my life studying the systems that shape our behavior. My background spans community organizing, venue operations, systems design, and product development grounded in lived environments.

Expertise

  • Community organizing & venue operations
  • Systems and ontology design
  • Cooperative governance and finance
  • Product development for real-world use
"Systems shape behavior, incentives matter more than intentions, and good infrastructure is mostly invisible."

Philosophy

I build for the long term, for real use, and for people who live with the consequences. My approach is interdisciplinary, starting with real use cases rather than abstractions.

Based in Boulder, Colorado, I work with people and organizations worldwide who are serious about building alternatives to extractive platforms.

I design for maintainability and stewardship, centering the people who live within the systems I build.
