Ideas & Frameworks

How I See
The Problem

Most modern crises are coordination failures, not moral ones.

Infrastructure vs Media

Social media optimizes for attention. Social infrastructure optimizes for function. Communities don't need more visibility—they need better ways to organize labor, care, and resources.

The Bottleneck

We have the talent and technology. What we lack are systems that make participation legible and align incentives without coercion.

"Local context is not noise. It's the signal."

Core Tenets

Respect Place

Technology should be place-aware. I design systems that honor geographic, cultural, and ecological boundaries.

Repair Over Disruption

I'm interested in repairing institutions and restoring capacity rather than "moving fast and breaking things."
