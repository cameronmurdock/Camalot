On systems and coordination

Notes

Most of what I write starts from observation rather than theory.

Patterns I keep running into:

  • — systems optimized for visibility instead of use
  • — tools that scale activity but not responsibility
  • — abstractions that erase local context

I’m interested in design that resists those tendencies without overcorrecting into ideology.

This page holds working ideas, not positions.
